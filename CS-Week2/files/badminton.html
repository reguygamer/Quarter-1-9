Badminton

● Badminton is a singles or doubles game. In singles, one player plays against another. In doubles, two players play against two others.

● The objective of the game is to hit the shuttlecock over the net and into your opponent's half of the court so that they cannot return it.

● The shuttlecock must be hit with the racquet before it bounces twice on the ground.

● A point is scored when the shuttlecock lands in your opponent's half of the court, or if your opponent fails to return the shuttlecock before it bounces twice.

● The first player or team to win 21 points wins the game, provided they have a 2-point lead. If the score is tied at 20-all, the game continues until one player or team has a 2-point lead.

● Badminton is a physically demanding sport that requires agility, speed, and hand-eye coordination. It is also a very social sport that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels.
